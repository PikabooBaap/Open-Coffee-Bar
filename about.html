About Us

Brewed with Love in Bharatpur

More than just a coffee shop — a friendly corner where the city comes to relax.

Our Story

Open Coffee Bar started with a simple idea: give Bharatpur a warm, welcoming place to enjoy a great cup of coffee and tasty food without any fuss. Sitting right on NH 21, Nagla Chandwari Road, we've become a favourite stop for students, families, travellers and anyone craving a comforting bite.

Whether it's an early-morning coffee, an afternoon plate of momos, or a Chinese dinner with friends, we put care into everything we serve.
